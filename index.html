ABOUT

The Sensitive Project is an ongoing artistic research work exploring perception as a constructed system.

Located at the intersection of fashion, photography, astrology, and critical visual theory, this project originates from a disrupted relationship to vision—fragmented, hyper-analytical, and shaped by lived experience.

Rather than representing bodies, The Sensitive Project attempts to reconstruct ways of seeing them through a practice called body reading.

Research Interests

  • Perception after trauma and visual distortion
  • Symbolic systems as tools for thinking differently
  • Ownership of the gaze and non-normative representation
  • Synesthetic translation (material, animal, floral)
  • Minor mythological figures as functional lenses
